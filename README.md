# Bipolar II Mood & Pattern Dashboard

A pyschology-focused behavioral tracking dashboard designed to help users monitor mood, sleep, energy, and behavioral patterns over time while providing educational resources about Bipolar II disorder.

The platform visualizes emotional treends through interactive analytics and promotes structured self-awareness through non-diagnostic mental health tracking.

---

## Overview

This prokect explores the intersection of pyschology, mental health awareness, and technology by providing users with tools to:
- Track daily mood and emotional patterns
- Monitor sleep and energy fluctuations
- Log behavioral sympyoms and personal notes
- Visualize long-term trends through data charts
- Learn about Bipolar II disorder and related psychological concepts

  ---

  ## Features

  ### Daily Mood Logging
  Users can submit daily entries including
  - Mood rating
  - Hours slept
  - Energy level
  - Anxiety/Irritability level
  - Personal notes

  ### Data Visualization Dashboard
  Interactive charts display:
  - Mood trends over time
  - Sleep patterns
  - Energy fluctuations
  - Weekly/monthly averagres
 
  ### Educational Resources
  Integrated informational sections provide:
  - Overview of Bipolar II disorder  
  - Explanation of hypomania vs depression  
  - Importance of sleep and routine regulation  
  - Mental health awareness resources  

  ### Pattern Recognition
  Users can identify:
  - Behavioral trends  
  - Emotional fluctuations  
  - Lifestyle correlations with mood changes
